The Histories of Tacitus: Book 1

About

This project represents an attempt to provide a clean and easy to use digital edition with an integrated critical apparatus. The text has been drawn from the Oxford Critical Edition of C.D. Fisher and the Perseus Digital Library. The text was encoded according to the TEI and Digital Latin Library Guidelines for encoding the critical apparatus.
